Who We Are

Feiyah Action Network is a community-based organization dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability, indigenous rights, and youth empowerment across Kenya and beyond. Our mission is to inspire action and resilience in communities facing social, economic, and ecological challenges.

Our Vision

A just, sustainable, and inclusive future where every community has the power to protect its environment, culture, and people.

Our Story

Feiyah Action Network began as a grassroots initiative led by youth activists determined to make a difference in climate justice and indigenous representation. Over time, we have grown into a dynamic network that connects communities, educators, and changemakers through advocacy, digital storytelling, and sustainable programs.
