Welcome to Feiyah

We are a youth-led, indigenous community movement dedicated to advancing gender equality, ending FGM, and creating safe spaces for empowerment and leadership.

About Us

Feiyah Action Network works to amplify indigenous voices and youth leadership across Kenya and beyond. Our approach blends cultural heritage with modern advocacy to promote human rights, education, and sustainable development.
